Kill threatened, endangered, and extinct species with Wildlife Killer!

How to Play

You are given a starter weapon and 100 gold when signing up for the game. To battle, you must equip the weapon and kill the fish before the fish either kills you or your weapon durability dies out. Once you win a battle, you capture the fish and have the chance to earn gold. You may sell fish for gold at the shop. If you lose health during a battle, you will be charged gold to recover to full health. If your weapon loses durability, you will lose the battle.

Travelling

Each region has a different probability of species appearing. To travel, you can access the travel page on the navbar and then select the country on the page.
